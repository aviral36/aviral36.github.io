QuickMaths Help
QuickMaths helps you improve your calculation speed, crucial in various fields of industry like consulting and investment banking. It is helpful to those preparing for competitive exams like CAT/GRE/GMAT, where calculation speed matters.

There are two modes:

  • Estimate
    In this mode, you are required to provide your answer within 10% error limits of the actual answer.
    For eg: 45x11 = 500 is an acceptable answer.
  • Accurate
    In this mode, you are required to give precise answer to the problem.
    For eg: 12x6 = 72 is the only acceptable answer.
    For division, in case of a decimal solution, you need to provide the greatest integer number preceding the decimal. For eg: The solution of 10/3 = [3.3333] will be written as 3, and 20/3 = [6.6666] will be written as 6.

There are 4 categories:

  • Addition (+)
    Problems related to addition of two numbers.
  • Subtraction (-)
    Problems related to subtraction of two numbers.
  • Multiplication (x)
    Problems related to multiplication of two numbers.
  • Division (รท)
    Problems related to division of two numbers.

There are 3 difficulty levels:

  • Easy
  • Medium
  • Hard
    • The difficulty levels are self explanatory.

Two minutes will be given to solve as many problems you can. For every correct answer, 3 marks will be awarded, and for every incorrect answer, 1 mark will be deducted from your total score. There is no provision to skip a question without answering.
